About Section

Finding the optimal time to release a movie is a pivotal component to the overall financial success of a movie. The overall research objective is to predict the ideal time for a studio to release their movie. Game Theory and Herfindahl Index are applied to develop a model with publicly available data from boxofficemojo.com. Essentially, predicting the ideal time for a studio to release a movie can potentially stimulate financial growth for a variety of movie studios.
